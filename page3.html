Not all those ideas work...

- Tell the kids to be quiet every five minutes
- Completely ignore them and continue on with the lesson hoping the other students do the same
- Send the kids out of the classroom when they are disruptive
Depending on the situation, some of those ideas might work but some will not. Ex. Ignoring the behavior might work for the moment but might also give the students the idea that you don't care how they act and could cause them to continue on with their disruprive behavior.
